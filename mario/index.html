Mario Bros. Play as dynamic duo Mario and Luigi and rid New York’s sewers of giant mutant turtles, crabs, and flies.

    
During the title sequence, press “mode” to choose a mode, and press “play” to begin play.

    
During play, press the red buttons or qwe on a keyboard to move Mario; press the green buttons or bnm on a keyboard to move Luigi.
